About MTANDA

East Africa's Most
Trusted Trade Partner

MTANDA operated by Ceres Holdings Limited is Uganda's premier trade distribution, supply chain and market development company. Since 2015, we have been the bridge between manufacturers and consumers, building markets that matter.

Our Story

Born in Uganda.
Built for Africa.

MTANDA was founded with a bold conviction: that Uganda's markets and the millions of people who depend on them deserve world-class distribution infrastructure. Not eventually. Now.

The name MTANDA captures our essence a network of connections, routes and relationships that bring products to people. Our parent company, Ceres Holdings Limited, is registered in Uganda and committed to building enterprises that create lasting economic value across East Africa.

From a founding team of 12 professionals in a single Kampala office, we have grown into an organisation of 500+ people operating across all 135 districts of Uganda with ambitions that extend across the entire East African Community.

Our Journey

10 Years of Building Uganda

From a startup with a big vision to East Africa's most comprehensive trade distribution partner our decade of growth reflects the potential of Uganda's markets when supported by the right infrastructure.

Awards & Recognition

  • ★ Uganda Business Council — Best Distribution Company 2023
  • ★ EABC Supply Chain Excellence Award 2022
  • ★ Uganda Revenue Authority — Top Taxpayer Recognition 2021–2024
  • ★ FMCG Uganda — Preferred Distributor of the Year 2020–2024
  • ★ Great Place to Work — Uganda Certified 2023
2015 — Foundation
Ceres Holdings Limited incorporated
MTANDA launched from a single Kampala office with 12 co-founders, 3 vehicles, and contracts to distribute for 4 manufacturers. The founding ethos: execute better than anyone else.
2016 — First Multinational Partner
Signed first FMCG multinational contract
A landmark partnership with a leading consumer goods company validated our model and accelerated growth. Fleet expanded to 25 vehicles. Team grew to 80 staff covering Central Region.
2017 — Regional Hubs
Opened Gulu, Mbale, and Mbarara hubs
Extended coverage to 40 districts. Established 3 regional distribution hubs with dedicated teams. Revenue grew 180% year-over-year. First warehousing facility opened in Kampala (20,000 sq ft).
2019 — Digital Transformation
Launched mobile-first field force platform
Deployed a proprietary mobile SFA platform to 200 field reps. Introduced real-time GPS tracking, digital order capture and BI dashboards — making MTANDA the most tech-enabled distributor in Uganda.
2021 — National Coverage
All 135 districts operational
Achieved complete national distribution coverage. Opened 8 regional hubs, grew fleet to 200+ GPS-tracked vehicles, warehousing to 100,000+ sq ft. Team of 500 professionals deployed nationwide.
2023 — Market Leadership
Recognised as Uganda's #1 distributor
200+ active partner brands, 50,000+ retail outlets, 2.4M tons distributed annually. Awarded Best Distribution Company by Uganda Business Council. East Africa expansion strategy launched.
2025 — Future Forward
Pan-African ambitions, AI-powered operations
Piloting AI-driven demand forecasting and route optimisation. Entering Kenya and Tanzania markets. Launching MTANDA Retail Credit — empowering 10,000+ retailers with working capital access.

Technology-Led Operations

Proprietary mobile SFA, real-time GPS fleet tracking, WMS and BI analytics dashboard. Our technology stack is purpose-built for East African market conditions.

Market Intelligence Engine

Weekly sell-through reports, competitor coverage analysis, consumer sentiment tracking and route-to-market optimization delivered to every partner in real time.

True National Footprint

135+ districts, 8 regional hubs, 200+ GPS-tracked vehicles. No partner needs a second distributor to cover Uganda MTANDA does it all, everywhere.

Dedicated Field Teams
