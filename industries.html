We are not generalists. We have built specialised competencies in each industry we serve from sector-specific regulations to unique channel dynamics and consumer behaviours.

FMCG & Consumer Goods
Uganda's largest and most competitive distribution sector. MTANDA handles distribution for some of the world's most recognisable consumer brands — food, beverages, personal care and household products — ensuring shelf presence in 50,000+ outlets nationwide.
Key Challenge We Solve

Achieving consistent national distribution without losing sell-through in secondary and tertiary markets. MTANDA's territory-based reps ensure that even rural district capitals receive weekly service, not just major urban centres.

Telecommunications
MTANDA is the last-mile distribution partner for Uganda's leading telecoms — managing airtime, data bundles, mobile devices and SIM card distribution across 50,000+ outlets. Our field teams ensure SIM registration compliance and product availability in every corner of the country.
Key Challenge We Solve

Ensuring consistent airtime and data availability in rural Uganda where digital payment infrastructure is limited. MTANDA bridges the gap with physical distribution complemented by digital ordering systems for rural agents.
