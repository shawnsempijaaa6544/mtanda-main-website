Mastering the Last Mile

Moving products from Kampala to regional hubs is the easy part. The true test of a distributor in Uganda is the last mile getting products onto the shelves of rural retail shops consistently and profitably.

  • Tuk-Tuk & Boda Fleet

    For dense urban slums and hard-to-reach rural terrain, our secondary fleet of three-wheelers and motorcycles ensures delivery where 3-ton trucks cannot go.

  • Mobile Order Capture

    Our field reps map GPS coordinates of every rural duka (shop), capturing orders digitally offline, which sync instantly upon reaching network coverage.

  • Micro-Routing Intelligence

    Dynamic routing algorithms cluster rural deliveries to ensure route profitability, even when serving small retail outlets.
