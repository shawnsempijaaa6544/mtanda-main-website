About MTANDA

East Africa's Most
Trusted Trade Partner

Founded as Ceres Holdings Limited, MTANDA was born from a simple but powerful idea: Uganda's markets deserve world-class distribution. Since our founding, we have grown from a regional distributor into East Africa's most comprehensive trade execution and supply chain partner.

Today, MTANDA operates across all 135 districts of Uganda with an integrated team of field specialists, logistics professionals, and market development experts who bring unmatched execution capability to every partnership.

Precision Execution
Every order delivered with accuracy, speed and full chain-of-custody visibility.
Trusted Partnerships
Long-term relationships built on transparency, reliability and shared growth.
Market Intelligence
Data-driven insights that transform distribution into competitive advantage.
Pan-African Reach
Infrastructure and ambition to scale across East Africa's emerging markets.

Our Mission

To build efficient, transparent and intelligent trade ecosystems that connect manufacturers to consumers creating economic value for all stakeholders across the supply chain.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and technologically advanced trade distribution company across East and Central Africa setting the standard for supply chain excellence.

Our Journey

2015 — Founded
Ceres Holdings Limited established
MTANDA launched operations in Kampala with a founding team of 12 professionals and a clear vision for transforming Uganda's distribution landscape.
2017 — Regional Expansion
Expanded to 40 districts
Opened regional hubs in Gulu, Mbale, and Mbarara. Grew to 150+ staff and onboarded our first FMCG multinational clients.
2019 — Technology Leap
Launched digital field force management
Deployed proprietary mobile-based sales force automation, real-time GPS tracking and BI dashboard — a first in Uganda's distribution sector.
2021 — National Coverage
All 135 districts covered
Achieved full national coverage. Grew fleet to 200+ vehicles, established 100,000+ sq ft of warehousing and expanded team to 500+ professionals.
2023 — Market Leadership
Uganda's #1 trade distribution company
Recognized as the market leader in trade distribution. Launched East Africa expansion and onboarded 50+ new manufacturer partnerships.
Today — East Africa's Trade Partner
50,000+ retail outlets served
Serving 200+ partners, managing 50,000+ retail outlets and moving over 2.4 million tons of products annually across the region.
